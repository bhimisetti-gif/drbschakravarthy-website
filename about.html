About

Dr. B. S. Chakravarthy

Professor, pediatrician, neonatologist, teacher, researcher and digital health innovator — committed to child health and to the deeper question of what it means to care for human life.

Professional identity

Dr. B. S. Chakravarthy serves as Professor & Head, Department of Pediatrics, Andhra Medical College & King George Hospital, Visakhapatnam.

His clinical practice began in 2003 at Latha Ganesh Children Clinic and continues to serve children and families in Visakhapatnam.

Clinical practice: Since 2003

Academic role: Professor & Head, Pediatrics

Focus: Pediatrics, Neonatology, Teaching, Research, Digital Health

Philosophy of care

Every child represents a future. Pediatric care is therefore not only the treatment of disease, but the protection of growth, development, dignity, family confidence and human possibility.

This website brings together clinical care, teaching, research, digital health and reflection in one place.
