⚠️ When to seek care immediately

  • Fast or difficult breathing
  • Refusal to feed or drink
  • Lethargy or unresponsiveness
  • Persistent high fever in an infant
  • Seizures or convulsions
  • Yellowing of skin in the first days of life
  • Bluish lips or skin
  • Signs of dehydration
