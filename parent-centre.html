Parent Guidance

Child health topics

🌡️ Fever & Common Illness

When fever is routine, and when it needs urgent attention. Home care, ORS, cough and danger signs.

💉 Vaccination

The national immunization schedule explained simply — birth to 5-year schedule, catch-up doses and common reactions.

📈 Growth & Nutrition

Reading a growth chart, complementary feeding, picky eating and understanding weight and height tracking.

👶 Newborn Care

Exclusive breastfeeding, cord care, keeping baby warm, jaundice watch and the first weeks at home.

🧩 Development

Motor, speech and social milestones, when to seek evaluation, and autism early signs — use Sisu Vikas for a detailed guide.

⚠️ Danger Signs

Fast or difficult breathing, refusal to feed, lethargy, seizures, bluish lips or persistent high fever — seek care immediately.
