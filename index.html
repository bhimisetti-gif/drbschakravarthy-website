Services

Care for every stage of childhood

Newborn Care

Jaundice guidance, feeding support, weight follow-up and newborn danger-sign counselling.

Vaccination

Routine immunization advice, missed-dose planning and parent-friendly vaccine counselling.

Growth & Nutrition

Growth monitoring, feeding advice, complementary feeding and nutrition guidance.

Development

Milestone review, developmental red flags and early referral guidance when needed.

Childhood Illness

Fever, cough, diarrhea, asthma, allergy and common pediatric infections.

High-risk Follow-up

Follow-up support for premature and high-risk babies after discharge.
