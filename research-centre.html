Research Projects

Current and recent work

4M Kids Trial — Pediatric TB

Active
ICMR–NIRT Funded Principal Investigator: Dr. B. S. Chakravarthy

A multi-site trial addressing diagnosis and management pathways in pediatric tuberculosis, including a nested caregiver burden sub-study.

Collaborators: Dr. Azger Dusthackeer (ICMR), Dr. Karikaalan

PNEUBEVAX-14 — Pneumococcal Vaccine Trial

Published
Site PI: AMC/KGH Lancet Regional Health

A pneumococcal conjugate vaccine trial conducted at King George Hospital, with findings published in Lancet Regional Health – Southeast Asia.

Neonatal Digital Registry Study

Active
IEC Submitted Department of Pediatrics, AMC/KGH

A registry-based study capturing neonatal scoring and outcome data through the PaedCare KGH research app suite, supporting quality improvement and publication-ready datasets.

Co-investigators: Dr. Ratnakumari, Dr. Satyavani

PaedCare KGH Suite — Clinical Validation Studies

Active
15 Apps IEC-Aligned Protocols

Validation of fifteen neonatal and pediatric scoring tools (APGAR, SNAPPE-II, CRIB-II, New Ballard, Downe's, Silverman-Anderson and others) against standard clinical practice, with a universal de-identification module across all apps.
